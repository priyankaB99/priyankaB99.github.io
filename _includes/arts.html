Music and Dance

Indian Classical Music and Dance is a major part of how I work, code, and interact with the world.

When I first began learning Bharathanatyam, I never could have predicted that I'd be practicing it still, 14 years later, as a young woman profoundly transformed by every tap of my foot, stretch of my arm, and bend of my torso.

For me, the beauty of music and dance is its universality and spirituality that enables anyone--no matter their background--to connect with it. Simply put, my dedication to exploring and practicing music and dance constantly pushes me to be a more compassionate, thoughtful human being.
